Welcome to Cookie Events

Cookie Events is an event planning, management and coordination establishment with the creative capacity to design and execute an unforgettable event. Founded on an ideology to cut through complexity while making lasting impression, Cookie Events is dedicated to crafting refined, elegant and truly memorable wedding, big-bash birthday, private events, brunch and corporate events for both domestic and international clientele.

Our Mission

To contribute to our client’s success by providing excellent strategic marketing services, events management and decoration that fulfill their objectives, requirements, and foreseen needs and wants in the most cost-efficient manner.

In partnership with event planners, hotels, venues, agencies and staging customers, we create and execute thousands of events annually – in person, virtual and hybrid – of any size, at any venue, anywhere in the country. Our core values are embedded in our DNA, and our team members are innovators and collaborators at heart – highly trained experts that intuitively combine creative, production and technology services to deliver unparalleled service excellence for you.

Our Values

Professionalism

We are a customer-focused company, and we are committed to have an in-depth understanding of our client’s needs, go above and beyond in providing quality services.

Interity

We operate on the values of trust, honesty, and transparency, to both our clients and our stakeholders. It is ingrained in our work culture and environment.

Creativity

We strive to bring new ideas to the table and produce compelling, consistent, and creative work for our clients, making their goals our own. Your success is our success.
